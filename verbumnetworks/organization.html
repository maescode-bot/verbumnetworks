Organization

Verbum Networks, with its corporate office at the Dominican Institute, Ibadan, Nigeria, is a leading Internet Service Provider in the South-West of Nigeria. With a broad-based record of clients which cuts across corporate business organizations, homes, government establishments, educational institutions, individual and other Internet service providers, Verbum has not only wielded to itself an exceptionally fast growing clientele, it is on its way to redefining information technology delivery and performance. With its motto of creativity, innovation and performance, Verbum has set a unique business standards of efficiency of delivery, reliability, competitive pricing and transparency. The company is manned by an exceptionally talented team of seasoned professionals.
